Pastel frito


Pastel is a typical brazilian street food that consists on crispy fried dough with a varied assortment of fillings.
it can be found on bars and bakeries sporadically but are normally sold on dedicated shops and street stalls.

Ingredients (15 portions):

  • 3 cups of wheat flour
  • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 soup spoon of salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of cane liquor
  • Wheat flour to work with the dough
  • Fillings to taste
    (Tip: you can start with seasoned minced meat!!!)

Cooking steps:

  1. Put the flour mixed with salt on a bowl or table a make a hole in the center.
  2. On that hole, mix the oil, cane liquor and a bit of water.
  3. Mix the water and the flour slowly going steadly to the border of that hole.
  4. When the dough starts getting stiff, add more water.
  5. The dough must get fluffy, if it gets a bit sticky, there is is no problem.
  6. If it gets too sticky, add flour and if it gets stiff, add water
  7. Add flour to a surface and, with the assistance of a rolling pin, start stretching the dough until it gets very thin (it won't fry well otherwise).
  8. Cut the dough in a size and shape of choice, add fillings on taste and fry.
