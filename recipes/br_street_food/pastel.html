Pastel frito


Pastel is a typical brazilian street food that consists on crispy fried dough with a varied assortment of fillings.
it can be found on bars and bakeries sporadically but are normally sold on dedicated shops and street stalls.

Ingredients (15 portions):

  • 3 cups of wheat flour
  • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 soup spoon of salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of cane liquor
  • Wheat flour to work with the dough
  • Fillings to taste
    (Tip: you can start with seasoned minced meat!!!)
