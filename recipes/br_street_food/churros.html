Churros frito


Churros is a loved brazilian sweet, especially among children, as such, this sweet can be commonly found on festival stalls, children parties and moving stall.
This sweet can be found in a varied assosrtment of fillings, altough the most commonly found ones are dulce de leche and chocolate.

Ingredients (20 portions):

  • 1 and 1/2 tea cups milk
  • 1/2 tea cups of water
  • 2 soup spoon of butter
  • 2 tea cups of wheat flour
  • salt to taste
  • sugar to taste
  • cinnamon to taste
  • Fillings on cook basis
    (Warning: to inject fillings you will need to hollow the dough yourself and apply with a syringe!!!
    Tip: you can start with chocolate or dulce de leche!!!)

Cooking steps:

  1. on a pot, add the milk, water, butter and salt.
  2. when the milk boil, add the flour and mix until the dough gets free from the pot bottom.
  3. put the dough on a pastry bag with apropritae beak and fry the resulting strips.
  4. mix the cinnamon and sugar and add it to the fried dough.
  5. hollow the dough and add the filling of taste.
