Coxinha frita with potato dough


Coxinha is a famous snack in brazil and is commonly seen in street stalls, bars, bakeries and parties.
Although the most common flavor of coxinha being chicken, the dough go pretty well with several diferrent ingredients, so experimentation is welcomed with this recipe.

Ingredients (100 portions):

  • 500 ml of milk
  • 100 grams of unsalted butter
  • 600 grams of wheat flour
  • 1 medium sized chicken breast cut
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500 grams of breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablets of chicken brooth in cubes
  • 400 grams of potatoes after being cooked and mashed
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 1 diced onion
  • half a pack of coriander
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 liter of cold water
  • 1 liter of cooking oil to fry

Cooking steps:

  1. cook the chicken breast with the 3 cups of water, bay leaf, one tablet of chicken brooth and diced onion.
  2. Shred the chicken.
  3. Mix until you get a smooth homogenous dough.
  4. Mix the shredded chicken, coriander, spoon of butter, cups of flour, tablet of chicken brooth and reserve it for later.
  5. On a pot add the milk and 500 ml of chicken brooth and let it boil.
  6. Add the already boiled and mashed potatoes, the the flour, until it gets loose from the bottom of the pot.
  7. put the dough on a surface to cool down, then stretch the dough a bit and model the dough.
  8. put it on cold water then breadcrumbs and fry.
