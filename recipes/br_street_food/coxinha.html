Coxinha frita


Coxinha is a famous snack in brazil and is commonly seen in street stalls, bars, bakeries and parties.
Although the most common flavor of coxinha being chicken, the dough go pretty well with several diferrent ingredients, so experimentation is welcome with this recipe.

Ingredients (100 portions):

  • 500 ml of milk
  • 100 grams of unsalted butter
  • 600 grams of wheat flour
  • 1 medium sized chicken breast cut
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500 grams of breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablets of chicken brooth in cubes
  • 400 grams of potatos after being cooked and mashed
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 1 diced onion
  • half a pack of coriander
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 liter of cold water
  • 1 liter of cooking oil to fry
